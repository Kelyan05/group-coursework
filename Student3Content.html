Physical Health

Keeping checks on your physical health consists of factors such as: Maintaining regular exercise; a healthy diet; upholding sufficent levels of sleep, and the like. Having a balanced diet, going to the gym— physical exercise in general— and aiming to keep a well balanced sleep schedule will keep your physical health in check.

Mental Health

Mental Health should not be neglected at all. Being able to manage your stress, asking for help when you need it, handling pressures from education, work or family life is a major part of being able to maintain peace of mind. Methods such as therapy, staying in contact with your close friends at a reasonable level, and generally keeping up with your hobbies and making sure you are doing what you want to do can keep your mental health at a well pace, however the argument of mental health significantly varies from person to person, as everyone has their own diffiuclties, and you shouldn't be afraid to reach out to whoever you can if you need it.

Social well-being

Finding and building social relationships between other people is also neccessary to lead a happy life. It will allow you to further maintain your Mental Health— having people to lean upon when you need it and knowing they're there for you— and your Physical Health, if you choose to interact with those people such as making plans with them, going to the gym with them, being able to learn things and grow with each other, all of these things make maintaining a healthy social life extremely important to maintaining Mental and Physical health, as well as good health in general.
