Building Connections

Building Connections is also extremely important for keeping your mental health in check. This should be done as early as possible, building connections when you are young and meeting new people constantly will help create friendships that will last a life time. Maintaining a good relationship with your family is more important however, those people are
the ones guaranteed to be there through the worst of situations within your life, and upholding that connection will always allow you to have something to lean towards when you need it most.

Making Plans

Building Connections ties into this final factor of being able to Make Plans with those people who you have formed connections with. Staying in touch with people in general, especially when you are feeling down or alone can
be extremely beneficial to your mental health. Generally, being with the people you like and have formed a connection with will give off more positive feeligns than negative, which, especially when you are not doing very well, can help immensely with preserving your mental Well-being
