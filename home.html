GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

This website's mission aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3. Our goal is to raise awareness into the lack of healthcare globally and to ensure healthy livesâ€” promoting the well being for everyone regardeless of age. We believe everyone deserves access to quality healthcare regardless of background. The United Nations strives to create a world where good health care is not a privilege but a right.

The Sustainable Development Goals make a bold commitment to end the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria and other communicable diseases by the year 2030. The aim is to achieve universal health coverage, and provide access to safe and affordable medicines and vaccines for all. To overcome these setbacks and address long-standing health care shortcomings, increased investment in health systems is needed to support countries in their recovery and build resilience against future health threats.

How can you help?

By taking charge of your well-being through making informed choices, prioritising healthy habits as well as ensuring your loved on are up-to-date on vaccinations. Educating others about healthy lifestyles and advocating for everyone's right to quality healthcare, especially for those who need it most. The United Nations urges you to take action; to hold your government and leaders accountable for their promises, and to improve access to healthcare services for everyone.
